Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (marinated)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices (turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala)
  • Fresh mint and coriander leaves
  • Saffron milk (optional)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook rice until it is 70% done and drain the water.
  3. Fry the onions and cook the marinated chicken until it's tender.
  4. Layer chicken and rice, add herbs and saffron milk on top.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 20â€“25 minutes. Serve hot.

Enjoy your meal!
