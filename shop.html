About Us

  • Welcome to The Tea and Coffee Co., your cozy destination for aromatic brews and warm vibes since 2015.

  • We began as a small neighborhood café and have now grown to serve tea lovers across the country with branches all over India.

  • Our shop is especially known for our signature Masala Chai — a perfect blend of spices, warmth, and tradition keeps On.

  • Whether you're here for a quiet cup of tea or a catch-up with friends over coffee, we welcome you to relax and sip in style!
